History

Shree Pragya Mrigi Rog Nivarak Samiti was established on September 21, 1978. The idea of opening this organisation is inspired by Late Shree 1008 Shree Kudndanmal Ji Maharaj Sa and in memory of Late Shree 1008 Shree PannaLal ji Maharaj Sa.

The Organisation is serving the patients of Epilepsy from Last 36 Years And Successful in providing satisfactory results. It was initiated with four patients, And it is a momonet of great pride that organisation is grown up with total 36000 patients till date and are medicated with full care which is extremely tremendous. According to a Survey, 80% of the patients are cured of it and living a happy life. Approximately 4500 patients every month are taking the treatment and getting fruitful results. People from different States including Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Punjab, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Gujrat, Andra Pradesh, Haryana, Punjab, Bihar, Chattisgarh and from abroad are restoring their health.
